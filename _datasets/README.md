Folders contain:

* ``_tools``: tools to work with raw *.csv files.
* ``_old_vs``: old file versions.
* 3-letter folders: specific scripts to create raw *.csv files from _as is_ datasets:
** ``adr``: ADRES
** ``ene``: EnerNOC
** ``enl``: Enliten
** ``goi``: Goiener
** ``iss``: ISSDA
** ``kag``: Kaggle
** ``lcl``: Low Carbon London
** ``nee``: NEEA
** ``per``: PerMod
** ``por``: Portugal
** ``ref``: Refit
